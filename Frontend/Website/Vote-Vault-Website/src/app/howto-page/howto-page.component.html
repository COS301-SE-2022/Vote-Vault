How To Register:
To register for an upcoming election you can do one of following two things:

1. Go to your local voting station and let a voting official register you.
2. Register yourself through the VoteVault app.

To register yourself you need to download the VoteVault app and enter your name, surname and ID number. You will also need to scan the barcode on the back of your ID. After the scan is complete you can press the "Register Now" button.
